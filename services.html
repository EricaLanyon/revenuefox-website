Services

Senior marketing help for the work that actually needs to get done.

Revenue Fox & Co. works on the friction between marketing strategy, sales follow-up, systems, campaigns, reporting, and documentation. Engagements can be focused or flexible depending on what your team needs most.

Start here

Revenue Path Diagnostic

For teams that need to understand where marketing, sales, lifecycle, reporting, or systems are breaking down — before trying to fix anything.

Most friction in B2B revenue teams is not mysterious. It is a process nobody documented, a definition nobody agreed on, or a system that was configured for a business model that no longer exists. The diagnostic finds it.

You walk away with a clear, prioritized picture of where the problems are, why they exist, and what to address first — not a 40-page report full of recommendations nobody acts on.

What you get
  • Revenue path audit across strategy, process, and systems
  • Friction map showing where pipeline leaks and why
  • Lifecycle and handoff review
  • Reporting and visibility gap analysis
  • Prioritized recommendations
  • Documented action plan
Clarity of message

Positioning, Messaging & GTM Clarity

For teams that need sharper messaging, clearer differentiation, or a stronger story across the website, campaigns, and sales conversations.

Weak positioning is not always obvious until you try to explain what you do and the room goes quiet, or sales says the messaging does not land, or website visitors convert at a rate that suggests they are not sure if you are talking to them.

This work gets into what makes the product or service genuinely different, who the right buyers are, what they actually care about, and how to tell the story in a way that moves people toward a conversation.

What you get
  • ICP and audience clarification
  • Positioning recommendations
  • Messaging framework and hierarchy
  • Value proposition refinement
  • Website messaging direction
  • Sales narrative support
Programs that run

Campaign, Lifecycle & Demand Programs

For teams that need practical campaign strategy and execution support across demand generation, nurture, lifecycle, ABM, or content programs.

Campaigns work better when they are connected to a lifecycle, a handoff process, and a reporting framework that tells you what is actually happening. This work builds or improves those connections — not just the campaign itself.

The output is programs that are planned, documented, and handed off in a form the team can run and improve without starting from scratch next quarter.

What you get
  • Campaign strategy and planning
  • Content and offer planning
  • Nurture and lifecycle flows
  • ABM or segment campaign planning
  • Email and landing page direction
  • Campaign reporting framework
Systems that match reality

HubSpot, Salesforce & Revenue Process Alignment

For teams whose systems do not match the way the business actually needs to operate — or for teams paying for HubSpot and Salesforce and not getting the value they expected.

The problem is almost never the tool. It is that the tool was configured around a process nobody agreed on, or a business model that has since changed, or by someone who is no longer there to explain why things were set up that way.

This work starts with the business process and revenue goals — not the tool settings — and builds a system that reflects how the company actually sells and markets.

What you get
  • Lead lifecycle definitions
  • MQL, SQL, SAL, and stage alignment between teams
  • Handoff process documentation
  • HubSpot and Salesforce process recommendations
  • Campaign tracking structure
  • Reporting cleanup recommendations
Full-stack marketing support

Broader B2B Marketing Support

Sometimes the problem is not one neat category. You may need a senior marketing partner who can step in, make sense of what is happening, and help move the right work forward.

Revenue Fox & Co. is built around fixing the friction between marketing, sales, and systems — but that is not a limitation. It is a starting point. Most marketing problems touch strategy, process, systems, and execution all at once, and the ability to see across all of them is what makes the work useful.

When a team needs hands-on marketing support that does not fit neatly into one of the categories above, that work is also in scope. The goal is always the same: clearer strategy, better execution, and work that is documented so the team can keep using it.

Support areas include
  • Marketing planning and strategy
  • Website copy and content direction
  • Sales enablement and alignment
  • Marketing operations support
  • Fractional marketing leadership
  • Project-based execution support
Engagement models

Flexible scope. Defined outcomes.

There is no bloated retainer, no upselling to a bigger team, and no open-ended engagement with no clear end state. Every engagement has a defined scope, stated outcomes, and documented deliverables. What varies is where we start.

Focused Diagnostic

A scoped project to find where the revenue path is breaking and produce a clear, prioritized action plan. Strong starting point for teams that are not sure exactly what needs to be fixed first.

Project-Based Engagement

A defined project with agreed scope, deliverables, and timeline. Covers one or more of the service areas above, delivered from start to finish with full documentation.

Fractional Advisory

Ongoing senior marketing thinking on a part-time basis. Right for teams that need consistent strategic input without a full-time hire — and want someone who understands the full revenue context.

Ongoing Execution Support

Hands-on support for teams that need consistent execution alongside strategy. Campaign work, systems improvements, documentation — with a senior person doing the work, not managing a junior team.

Not sure what you need yet? That is okay.

The best starting point is usually a conversation about what is actually happening — not a perfectly scoped project brief. Let's talk through what is going on and find the right place to start.
